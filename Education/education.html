Bachelor Degree of Political Science

Political Science is the study of government systems, political behavior, and public policies. It explores how power and authority are distributed, how decisions are made, and how political institutions function at local, national, and global levels. The field covers topics like democracy, law, international relations, political theory, and comparative politics.

High School Certificate

The High School Certificate (HSC) is awarded after the successful completion of higher secondary education, usually after 12th grade. It signifies the completion of advanced studies in streams like Science, Commerce, or Arts and is a key qualification for entry into universities or professional courses.

Secondary School Certificate

High School Secondary Education refers to the stage of education that typically includes grades 9 through 12, usually for students aged 14 to 18. It provides a broad academic foundation across subjects like math, science, language arts, and social studies, while also preparing students for higher education, vocational training, or entry into the workforce.
